Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

URL AI ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how the URL AI mobile application collects, uses, and protects user data.

1. Information We Collect

URL AI may collect the following information:

  • Target URLs entered by the user for monitoring
  • Conversation history stored locally on your device
  • Model settings and configuration preferences
  • Optional API keys entered by the user for supported AI services
  • Local model usage preferences such as GPU settings and tuning parameters

We do not collect personal identity information such as your name, phone number, or address unless you voluntarily provide it.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your information is used only for:

  • Providing AI-powered URL monitoring functionality
  • Saving conversation history and settings locally
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Supporting local model execution and optimization

3. Local Storage

Most app data, including conversations and settings, is stored locally on your device using secure local storage. This data is not automatically uploaded to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

If you choose to use external AI providers such as Gemini or other supported services in future versions, your requests may be processed by those third-party providers according to their own privacy policies.

In the current release, the app primarily supports local Gemma model usage for on-device processing.

5. API Keys

Any API keys entered into the app are stored locally on your device and are used only for the services you configure. We do not transmit or store your API keys on external servers.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your locally stored information. However, no system can guarantee complete security, and users are responsible for protecting access to their own devices.

8. Children's Privacy

URL AI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date on this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us through the app listing or developer contact information provided on Google Play.
